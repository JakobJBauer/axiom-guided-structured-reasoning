[THEME-DECEPTION]
A story is [THEME-DECEPTION] if The story has the theme Deception.

[HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY]
A story is [HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY] if The story has a story within a story.

[STYLE-SURREAL]
A story is [STYLE-SURREAL] if The story is surreal.

[THEME-HELPING-OTHERS]
A story is [THEME-HELPING-OTHERS] if The story has the theme Helping Others.

[STYLE-MINIMALIST]
A story is [STYLE-MINIMALIST] if The story is minimalist.

[THEME-GROWTH]
A story is [THEME-GROWTH] if The story has the theme Growth.

[STYLE-HUMOROUS]
A story is [STYLE-HUMOROUS] if The story is humorous.

[USES-DISCOURSE-MARKERS]
A story is [USES-DISCOURSE-MARKERS] if The story uses discourse markers.

[STARTS-WITH-NOUN]
A story is [STARTS-WITH-NOUN] if The story starts with a noun.

[HAS-CIRCULAR-NARRATIVE-STRUCT]
A story is [HAS-CIRCULAR-NARRATIVE-STRUCT] if The story has circular narrative structure.

[NARRATIVE-COMPLEXITY]
A story is [NARRATIVE-COMPLEXITY] if either of the following is true:
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY]
  - Exactly one of the following is true:
    - The story is [THEME-DECEPTION]
    - The story is [THEME-GROWTH]
- The story is [HAS-CIRCULAR-NARRATIVE-STRUCT] and the story is not [STARTS-WITH-NOUN]

[ENGAGING-STORY]
A story is [ENGAGING-STORY] if both of the following are true:
- The story is [NARRATIVE-COMPLEXITY]
- Either of the following is true:
  - The truth of [USES-DISCOURSE-MARKERS] equals the truth of [STARTS-WITH-NOUN] (both true or both false)
  - One of the following style nodes is in the story:
    - The story is [STYLE-SURREAL]
    - The story is [STYLE-HUMOROUS]
    - The story is [STYLE-MINIMALIST]
