[USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]
A story is [USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD] if it uses the conditional mood.

[USES-PERFECT-ASPECT]
A story is [USES-PERFECT-ASPECT] if it uses the perfect aspect.

[THEME-DECEPTION]
A story is [THEME-DECEPTION] if it has the theme Deception.

[HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER]
A story is [HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER] if it has a cliffhanger.

[COMPLEX-DECEPTION]
A story is [COMPLEX-DECEPTION] if all of the following are true:
- The story is [THEME-DECEPTION]
- The story is not [HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER]
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]
  - The story is [USES-PERFECT-ASPECT]

[ASPECTUAL-AMBIVALENCE]
A story is [ASPECTUAL-AMBIVALENCE] if both of the following are true:
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [USES-PERFECT-ASPECT]
  - The story is [USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]
- The story is not both of the following:
  - The story is [USES-PERFECT-ASPECT]
  - The story is [USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]

[AMBIGUOUS-DECEPTION-NARRATIVE]
A story is [AMBIGUOUS-DECEPTION-NARRATIVE] if both of the following are true:
- The story is [COMPLEX-DECEPTION]
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [ASPECTUAL-AMBIVALENCE]
  - The story is not [HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER]
