[HIGH-READABILITY]
A story is HIGH-READABILITY if The story has readability score above 96.1

[LOW-READABILITY]
A story is LOW-READABILITY if The story has readability score below 86.4

[STARTS-WITH-NOUN]
A story is STARTS-WITH-NOUN if The story starts with a noun

[STARTS-WITH-ADVERB]
A story is STARTS-WITH-ADVERB if The story starts with a adverb

[USES-PERFECT-ASPECT]
A story is USES-PERFECT-ASPECT if The story uses perfect aspect

[USES-DISCOURSE-MARKERS]
A story is USES-DISCOURSE-MARKERS if The story uses discourse markers

[HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER]
A story is HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER if The story has a cliffhanger

[HAS-JUXTAPOSITION]
A story is HAS-JUXTAPOSITION if The story has juxtaposition

[HAS-A-RED-HERRING]
A story is HAS-A-RED-HERRING if The story has a Red Herring

[SHORT-STORY]
A story is SHORT-STORY if The story has fewer than 160 words

[STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL]
A story is STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL if The story is philosophical

[STYLE-PLAYFUL]
A story is STYLE-PLAYFUL if The story is playful

[STYLE-HUMOROUS]
A story is STYLE-HUMOROUS if The story is humorous

[THEME-AGENCY]
A story is THEME-AGENCY if The story has the theme Agency

[OPENING-TONE]
A story is OPENING-TONE if both of the following are true:
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [STARTS-WITH-NOUN]
  - The story is [STARTS-WITH-ADVERB]
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL]
  - The story is [STYLE-PLAYFUL]

[NARRATIVE-PACE]
A story is NARRATIVE-PACE if either of the following is true:
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [SHORT-STORY]
  - The story is [HIGH-READABILITY]
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [USES-PERFECT-ASPECT]
  - The story is not [LOW-READABILITY]

[COMEDIC-STRUCTURE]
A story is COMEDIC-STRUCTURE if both of the following are true:
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [STYLE-PLAYFUL]
  - The story is [STYLE-HUMOROUS]
- Both of the following are true:
  - Exactly one of the following is true:
    - The story is [SHORT-STORY]
    - The story is [USES-DISCOURSE-MARKERS]
  - Either of the following is true:
    - The story is [HAS-A-RED-HERRING]
    - The story is [HAS-JUXTAPOSITION]

[THEMATIC-FUSION]
A story is THEMATIC-FUSION if both of the following are true:
- The story is [THEME-AGENCY]
- Either of the following is true:
  - Both of the following are true:
    - The story is [STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL]
    - The story is [OPENING-TONE]
  - Exactly one of the following is true:
    - The story is [HAS-JUXTAPOSITION]
    - The story is [HAS-A-RED-HERRING]

[RAPTURE-ENDING]
A story is RAPTURE-ENDING if either of the following is true:
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER]
  - The story is not [SHORT-STORY]
- Exactly one of the following is true:
  - The story is [HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER]
  - The story is [HAS-A-RED-HERRING]

[COMPLEX-STORY]
A story is COMPLEX-STORY if both of the following are true:
- All of the following are true:
  - The story is [THEMATIC-FUSION]
  - The story is [COMEDIC-STRUCTURE]
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [NARRATIVE-PACE]
  - Both of the following are true:
    - The story is [RAPTURE-ENDING]
    - The story is not [HIGH-READABILITY]
