[HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY]
Definition: The predicate [HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY] holds for a story if the narrative includes a nested or embedded story (i.e., the story contains a story within a story).

[THEME-MAGIC]
Definition: The predicate [THEME-MAGIC] holds for a story if the story’s thematic content is Magic.

[HAS-A-FLASHBACK]
Definition: The predicate [HAS-A-FLASHBACK] holds for a story if the narrative contains a flashback sequence.

[TOPIC-THE-ARTS]
Definition: The predicate [TOPIC-THE-ARTS] holds for a story if the story’s subject matter pertains to the arts.

[STYLE-MINIMALIST]
Definition: The predicate [STYLE-MINIMALIST] holds for a story if the story is written in a minimalist style.

[HAS-CIRCULAR-NARRATIVE-STRUCT]
Definition: The predicate [HAS-CIRCULAR-NARRATIVE-STRUCT] holds for a story if the narrative follows a circular structure.

[DEVICE-XOR]
Definition: The predicate [DEVICE-XOR] holds for a story if exactly one of the following conditions is true:
- The story is [HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY]
- The story is [HAS-A-FLASHBACK]

[DEVICE-EQUAL]
Definition: The predicate [DEVICE-EQUAL] holds for a story if the truth value of [HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY] is equal to the truth value of [HAS-CIRCULAR-NARRATIVE-STRUCT].

[MAGIC-ART-MEMBER]
Definition: The predicate [MAGIC-ART-MEMBER] holds for a story if the story’s primary theme or topic is a member of the set {[THEME-MAGIC], [TOPIC-THE-ARTS]}.

[COMPLEX-NARRATIVE-CORE]
Definition: The predicate [COMPLEX-NARRATIVE-CORE] holds for a story if both of the following conditions are satisfied:
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [DEVICE-XOR]
  - The story is [DEVICE-EQUAL]
- The story is not [STYLE-MINIMALIST] and (the story is [HAS-CIRCULAR-NARRATIVE-STRUCT] or the story is [HAS-A-FLASHBACK])

[ART-MAGIC-NARRATIVE]
Definition: The predicate [ART-MAGIC-NARRATIVE] holds for a story if both of the following conditions are satisfied:
- The story is [MAGIC-ART-MEMBER]
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [COMPLEX-NARRATIVE-CORE]
  - The story is [STYLE-MINIMALIST]

[COMPLEX-ARTISTIC-NARRATIVE]
Definition: The predicate [COMPLEX-ARTISTIC-NARRATIVE] holds for a story if both of the following conditions are satisfied:
- The story is [ART-MAGIC-NARRATIVE]
- Exactly one of the following is true:
  - The story is [DEVICE-EQUAL]
  - The story is not [HAS-A-FLASHBACK]
