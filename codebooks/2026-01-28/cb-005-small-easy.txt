[USES-DISCOURSE-MARKERS]
A story is USES-DISCOURSE-MARKERS if the story uses discourse markers

[HAS-JUXTAPOSITION]
A story is HAS-JUXTAPOSITION if the story has juxtaposition

[TOPIC-MYSTICAL-CREATURES]
A story is TOPIC-MYSTICAL-CREATURES if the story is about mystical creatures

[HAS-A-RED-HERRING]
A story is HAS-A-RED-HERRING if the story has a Red Herring

[TOPIC-GIANT-CREATURES]
A story is TOPIC-GIANT-CREATURES if the story is about giant creatures

[FANTASTICAL-JUXTAPOSITION]
A story is FANTASTICAL-JUXTAPOSITION if both of the following are true:
- The story is [TOPIC-MYSTICAL-CREATURES]
- The story is [HAS-JUXTAPOSITION]

[GIANT-MISDIRECTION]
A story is GIANT-MISDIRECTION if both of the following are true:
- The story is [TOPIC-GIANT-CREATURES]
- The story is [HAS-A-RED-HERRING]
