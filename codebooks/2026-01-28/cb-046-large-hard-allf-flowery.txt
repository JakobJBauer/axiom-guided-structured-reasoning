[STYLE-LIGHTHEARTED]
A story is [STYLE-LIGHTHEARTED] if the story is lighthearted — if its tone skims like sunlit laughter across a pond, the mood buoyant and untroubled.

[STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL]
A story is [STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL] if the story is philosophical — if it lingers over questions of meaning, pondering existence with the slow, patient curiosity of an old oak.

[TOPIC-MAGICAL-LANDS]
A story is [TOPIC-MAGICAL-LANDS] if the story is about magical lands — if its setting unfurls into realms where the horizon shimmers with enchantment.

[USES-WH-QUESTIONS]
A story is [USES-WH-QUESTIONS] if the story uses wh-questions — if it asks who, what, when, where, why, or how, inviting inquiry like a lantern in the dark.

[SHORT-STORY]
A story is [SHORT-STORY] if the story has fewer than 160 words — compact as a pressed flower, brief and concentrated.

[THEME-KINDNESS]
A story is [THEME-KINDNESS] if the story has the theme Kindness — if acts of compassion and gentle hearts are central to its tapestry.

[MANY-PARAGRAPHS]
A story is [MANY-PARAGRAPHS] if the story has more than 6 paragraphs — a sprawling manuscript of thought and scene, segmented into many breaths.

[USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]
A story is [USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD] if the story uses conditional mood — if it speaks in coulds and woulds, in the language of possibility and imagined consequence.

[THEME-PROBLEM-SOLVING]
A story is [THEME-PROBLEM-SOLVING] if the story has the theme Problem-Solving — if challenges are met with cleverness and the spirit of resolution.

[THEME-GROWTH]
A story is [THEME-GROWTH] if the story has the theme Growth — if characters or ideas stretch, transform, or bloom as the plot progresses.

[STARTS-WITH-PREPOSITION]
A story is [STARTS-WITH-PREPOSITION] if the story starts with a preposition — if the very first word is a prepositional thread that ties the opening to place or relation.

[TOPIC-GIANT-CREATURES]
A story is [TOPIC-GIANT-CREATURES] if the story is about giant creatures — if colossal beings stride through its landscapes and influence fate by their sheer magnitude.

[STYLE-MODERN]
A story is [STYLE-MODERN] if the story is modern — if it reflects contemporary sensibilities, voice, or settings.

[STYLE-CLASSIC]
A story is [STYLE-CLASSIC] if the story is classic — if it resonates with timeless style, form, or archetypal cadence.

[THEME-MAGIC]
A story is [THEME-MAGIC] if the story has the theme Magic — if the weave of the tale is threaded through with sorcery, wonder, or inexplicable power.

[TOPIC-LOST-CIVILIZATIONS]
A story is [TOPIC-LOST-CIVILIZATIONS] if the story is about lost civilizations — if it explores ruins, vanished peoples, and the echoes of bygone societies.

[HAS-A-FLASHBACK]
A story is [HAS-A-FLASHBACK] if the story has a flashback — if a past moment is summoned into the present to shed light on why things are as they are.

[TOPIC-HIDDEN-TREASURES]
A story is [TOPIC-HIDDEN-TREASURES] if the story is about hidden treasures — if secret hoards, buried riches, or concealed wonders drive the narrative.

[NON-LIGHTHEARTED]
A story is [NON-LIGHTHEARTED] if the story is not [STYLE-LIGHTHEARTED] — if it lacks that feather-light levity and carries a more serious hue.

[MODERN-XOR-CLASSIC]
A story is [MODERN-XOR-CLASSIC] if exactly one of the following is true:
- The story is [STYLE-MODERN]
- The story is [STYLE-CLASSIC]
Here, the designation blooms only when the tale chooses a single stylistic path — modern or classic, but not both.

[MAGICAL-ADVENTURE]
A story is [MAGICAL-ADVENTURE] if both of the following are true:
- The story is [TOPIC-MAGICAL-LANDS]
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [THEME-MAGIC]
  - Both of the following are true:
    - The story is [TOPIC-GIANT-CREATURES]
    - The story is [THEME-PROBLEM-SOLVING]
This label belongs to those adventures that dwell in enchanted realms and are propelled either by magic itself, or by the interplay of towering beings and inventive problem-solving.

[HIDDEN-LOST-ADVENTURE]
A story is [HIDDEN-LOST-ADVENTURE] if either of the following is true:
- Exactly one of the following is true:
  - The story is [TOPIC-HIDDEN-TREASURES]
  - The story is [TOPIC-LOST-CIVILIZATIONS]
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [TOPIC-HIDDEN-TREASURES]
  - The story is [TOPIC-LOST-CIVILIZATIONS]
This designation captures tales of discovery — whether focused on secret hoards, forgotten empires, or the bittersweet confluence of both.

[QUEST-STYLE]
A story is [QUEST-STYLE] if both of the following are true:
- [USES-WH-QUESTIONS] is in the set of devices the story uses (it explicitly uses wh-questions)
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]
  - The story is [STARTS-WITH-PREPOSITION]
A quest-style tale is one that invites inquiry — it asks its way forward with wh-questions and proceeds either in the register of possibility (conditional mood) or with an opening prepositional step.

[EPIC-GROWTH-ARC]
A story is [EPIC-GROWTH-ARC] if both of the following are true:
- The set of themes in the story is equal to the set containing [THEME-KINDNESS] and [THEME-GROWTH]
- Either of the following is true:
  - Both of the following are true:
    - The story is [THEME-PROBLEM-SOLVING]
    - The story is [HAS-A-FLASHBACK]
  - Both of the following are true:
    - The story is [MANY-PARAGRAPHS]
    - The story is not [SHORT-STORY]
An epic growth arc is a rare bloom: a narrative whose thematic soil is precisely kindness and growth, and which either intertwines problem-solving with remembered past, or unfolds over many paragraphs without being a mere short-story.

[AMALGAM-OF-ADVENTURE]
A story is [AMALGAM-OF-ADVENTURE] if either of the following is true:
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [MAGICAL-ADVENTURE]
  - The story is [HIDDEN-LOST-ADVENTURE]
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [TOPIC-GIANT-CREATURES]
  - The story is [TOPIC-LOST-CIVILIZATIONS]
This label is given when adventures converge — when magic and lost quests entwine, or when giants and ancient ruins share the stage.

[AMBIGUOUS-MAGICAL-TALE]
A story is [AMBIGUOUS-MAGICAL-TALE] if either of the following is true:
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [AMALGAM-OF-ADVENTURE]
  - Both of the following are true:
    - The story is [MODERN-XOR-CLASSIC]
    - Either of the following is true:
      - The story is [THEME-KINDNESS]
      - The story is [THEME-MAGIC]
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [EPIC-GROWTH-ARC]
  - Both of the following are true:
    - The story is not [NON-LIGHTHEARTED]
    - The story is [QUEST-STYLE]
This final, evocative classification is reserved for tales perched on the cusp of interpretation — adventurous hybrids or epic growth narratives that remain delightfully uncertain, modern or classic in sole identity, suffused with kindness or magic, or questing forward without losing a lightness of heart.
