[SHORT-STORY]
A story is [SHORT-STORY] if it has fewer than 160 words.

[THEME-MAGIC]
A story is [THEME-MAGIC] if its theme is Magic.

[USES-WH-QUESTIONS]
A story is [USES-WH-QUESTIONS] if it uses wh-questions.

[HAS-CHECKHOVS-GUN]
A story is [HAS-CHECKHOVS-GUN] if it includes Checkhov's gun.

[HIGH-READABILITY]
A story is [HIGH-READABILITY] if its readability score is above 96.1.

[MYSTERIOUS-MAGIC]
A story is [MYSTERIOUS-MAGIC] if both of the following are true:
- The story is [THEME-MAGIC]
- The story is not [HAS-CHECKHOVS-GUN]

[TIGHT-MAGIC-CRAFT]
A story is [TIGHT-MAGIC-CRAFT] if both of the following are true:
- The story is [SHORT-STORY]
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [MYSTERIOUS-MAGIC]
  - Exactly one of the following is true:
    - The story is not [HIGH-READABILITY]
    - The story is [USES-WH-QUESTIONS]
