[TOPIC-CULTURAL-TRADITIONS]
A story is [TOPIC-CULTURAL-TRADITIONS] if the story is about cultural traditions.
Think of this tag as the home for stories that explore rituals, customs, festivals, or practices rooted in a community’s shared history—anything where culture and tradition are central to the narrative.

[HAS-A-MORAL-LESSON]
A story is [HAS-A-MORAL-LESSON] if the story has a moral lesson.
Use this when the tale clearly teaches or implies a lesson—an ethical takeaway or guiding principle the reader is meant to notice.

[STARTS-WITH-ADVERB]
A story is [STARTS-WITH-ADVERB] if the story starts with a adverb.
This flags openings that begin with an adverbial word (for example, “Quietly,” “Suddenly,” or “Unfortunately,”) setting tone or pace right from the first word.

[THEME-MAGIC]
A story is [THEME-MAGIC] if the story has the theme Magic.
Tag stories where magic—wonder, supernatural forces, spells, or mystical elements—is a clear thematic thread.
