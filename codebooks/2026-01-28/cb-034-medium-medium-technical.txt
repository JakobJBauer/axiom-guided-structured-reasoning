[THEME-GROWTH]
A story is classified as [THEME-GROWTH] if the narrative contains the thematic element "Growth."

[THEME-HELPING-OTHERS]
A story is classified as [THEME-HELPING-OTHERS] if the narrative contains the thematic element "Helping Others."

[STARTS-WITH-ADVERB]
A story is classified as [STARTS-WITH-ADVERB] if the first lexical token of the story is an adverb.

[STYLE-MODERN]
A story is classified as [STYLE-MODERN] if the narrative exhibits characteristics of modern style.

[TOPIC-SEASONAL-CHANGES]
A story is classified as [TOPIC-SEASONAL-CHANGES] if the subject matter concerns seasonal changes.

[USES-PERFECT-ASPECT]
A story is classified as [USES-PERFECT-ASPECT] if the narrative employs perfect aspect in verb constructions.

[LOW-READABILITY]
A story is classified as [LOW-READABILITY] if the computed readability score for the text is below 86.4.

[STYLE-CLASSIC]
A story is classified as [STYLE-CLASSIC] if the narrative exhibits characteristics of classic style.

[TOPIC-MYSTICAL-CREATURES]
A story is classified as [TOPIC-MYSTICAL-CREATURES] if the subject matter concerns mystical creatures.

[HIGH-READABILITY]
A story is classified as [HIGH-READABILITY] if the computed readability score for the text is above 96.1.

[SEASONAL-GROWTH]
A story is classified as [SEASONAL-GROWTH] if and only if all of the following conditions hold:
- The story is [TOPIC-SEASONAL-CHANGES]
- The story is [THEME-GROWTH]
- The story is not [TOPIC-MYSTICAL-CREATURES]

[COMPOSED-STORY]
A story is classified as [COMPOSED-STORY] if any one of the following disjunctive conditions is satisfied:

- Conjunctive condition A (both must be true):
  - The story is [SEASONAL-GROWTH]
  - The story is [THEME-HELPING-OTHERS]

- Disjunctive branch B (either of the following sub-branches):
  - Branch B1 (both must be true):
    - The story is [STYLE-MODERN]
    - The story is not [LOW-READABILITY]
  - Branch B2 (both must be true):
    - The story is [STYLE-CLASSIC]
    - Conjunctive requirement (both must be true):
      - The story is [USES-PERFECT-ASPECT]
      - The story is [HIGH-READABILITY]
