[HIGH-READABILITY]
A story is [HIGH-READABILITY] if the story's readability score is greater than 96.1.

[LOW-READABILITY]
A story is [LOW-READABILITY] if the story's readability score is less than 86.4.

[STARTS-WITH-NOUN]
A story is [STARTS-WITH-NOUN] if the first token of the story is a noun.

[STARTS-WITH-ADVERB]
A story is [STARTS-WITH-ADVERB] if the first token of the story is an adverb.

[USES-PERFECT-ASPECT]
A story is [USES-PERFECT-ASPECT] if the story contains at least one verb phrase in the perfect aspect.

[USES-DISCOURSE-MARKERS]
A story is [USES-DISCOURSE-MARKERS] if the text contains discourse markers (e.g., however, therefore, meanwhile).

[HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER]
A story is [HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER] if the narrative concludes with an unresolved or suspenseful ending (a cliffhanger).

[HAS-JUXTAPOSITION]
A story is [HAS-JUXTAPOSITION] if the narrative includes juxtaposition (contrast of elements or ideas placed in proximity).

[HAS-A-RED-HERRING]
A story is [HAS-A-RED-HERRING] if the narrative contains a deliberate misleading clue or distractor (a red herring).

[SHORT-STORY]
A story is [SHORT-STORY] if the total word count is fewer than 160 words.

[STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL]
A story is [STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL] if the style exhibits philosophical themes, tone, or argumentation.

[STYLE-PLAYFUL]
A story is [STYLE-PLAYFUL] if the style exhibits playfulness, lightness, or whimsical tone.

[STYLE-HUMOROUS]
A story is [STYLE-HUMOROUS] if the style is primarily intended to produce humor.

[THEME-AGENCY]
A story is [THEME-AGENCY] if the central theme concerns agency (control, autonomy, or action by agents).

[OPENING-TONE]
A story is [OPENING-TONE] if both of the following are true:
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [STARTS-WITH-NOUN]
  - The story is [STARTS-WITH-ADVERB]
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL]
  - The story is [STYLE-PLAYFUL]

[NARRATIVE-PACE]
A story is [NARRATIVE-PACE] if either of the following is true:
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [SHORT-STORY]
  - The story is [HIGH-READABILITY]
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [USES-PERFECT-ASPECT]
  - The story is not [LOW-READABILITY]

[COMEDIC-STRUCTURE]
A story is [COMEDIC-STRUCTURE] if both of the following are true:
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [STYLE-PLAYFUL]
  - The story is [STYLE-HUMOROUS]
- Both of the following are true:
  - Exactly one of the following is true:
    - The story is [SHORT-STORY]
    - The story is [USES-DISCOURSE-MARKERS]
  - Either of the following is true:
    - The story is [HAS-A-RED-HERRING]
    - The story is [HAS-JUXTAPOSITION]

[THEMATIC-FUSION]
A story is [THEMATIC-FUSION] if both of the following are true:
- The story is [THEME-AGENCY]
- Either of the following is true:
  - Both of the following are true:
    - The story is [STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL]
    - The story is [OPENING-TONE]
  - Exactly one of the following is true:
    - The story is [HAS-JUXTAPOSITION]
    - The story is [HAS-A-RED-HERRING]

[RAPTURE-ENDING]
A story is [RAPTURE-ENDING] if either of the following is true:
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER]
  - The story is not [SHORT-STORY]
- Exactly one of the following is true:
  - The story is [HAS-A-CLIFFHANGER]
  - The story is [HAS-A-RED-HERRING]

[COMPLEX-STORY]

- All of the following are true:

  - The story is [NARRATIVE-PACE]
