## Codebook (Structured, Logic-Preserving)

Use the following nodes exactly as defined. Each node applies **if and only if** its stated condition(s) hold. All bracketed node IDs must remain unchanged.

---

# 1) Base / Atomic Nodes (Direct Checks)

### Length & Formatting
- **[MANY-PARAGRAPHS]**
  - A story is **[MANY-PARAGRAPHS]** if the story has **more than 6 paragraphs**.
- **[LONG-STORY]**
  - A story is **[LONG-STORY]** if the story has **more than 375 words**.

### Readability
- **[LOW-READABILITY]**
  - A story is **[LOW-READABILITY]** if the story has **readability score below 86.4**.

### Narrative Devices & Structure
- **[HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY]**
  - A story is **[HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY]** if the story has **a story within a story**.
- **[HAS-CHECKHOVS-GUN]**
  - A story is **[HAS-CHECKHOVS-GUN]** if the story has **Checkhov's gun**.
- **[HAS-A-MORAL-LESSON]**
  - A story is **[HAS-A-MORAL-LESSON]** if the story has **a moral lesson**.

### Opening Token / First-Word Type
- **[STARTS-WITH-NOUN]**
  - A story is **[STARTS-WITH-NOUN]** if the story **starts with a noun**.
- **[STARTS-WITH-ADVERB]**
  - A story is **[STARTS-WITH-ADVERB]** if the story **starts with a adverb**.
- **[STARTS-WITH-ADJECTIVE]**
  - A story is **[STARTS-WITH-ADJECTIVE]** if the story **starts with a adjective**.
- **[STARTS-WITH-NONNOUN]**
  - A story is **[STARTS-WITH-NONNOUN]** if **it is not [STARTS-WITH-NOUN]**.

### Grammar & Questioning
- **[USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]**
  - A story is **[USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]** if the story **uses conditional mood**.
- **[USES-WH-QUESTIONS]**
  - A story is **[USES-WH-QUESTIONS]** if the story **uses wh-questions**.
- **[USES-SUPERLATIVE-FORMS]**
  - A story is **[USES-SUPERLATIVE-FORMS]** if the story **uses superlative forms**.

### Themes
- **[THEME-MAGIC]**
  - A story is **[THEME-MAGIC]** if the story has the theme **Magic**.
- **[THEME-INNOVATION]**
  - A story is **[THEME-INNOVATION]** if the story has the theme **Innovation**.
- **[THEME-GROWTH]**
  - A story is **[THEME-GROWTH]** if the story has the theme **Growth**.
- **[THEME-HARDSHIP]**
  - A story is **[THEME-HARDSHIP]** if the story has the theme **Hardship**.

### Topics
- **[TOPIC-HIDDEN-TREASURES]**
  - A story is **[TOPIC-HIDDEN-TREASURES]** if the story is about **hidden treasures**.
- **[TOPIC-SEASONAL-CHANGES]**
  - A story is **[TOPIC-SEASONAL-CHANGES]** if the story is about **seasonal changes**.
- **[TOPIC-THE-ARTS]**
  - A story is **[TOPIC-THE-ARTS]** if the story is about **the arts**.
- **[TOPIC-MAGICAL-LANDS]**
  - A story is **[TOPIC-MAGICAL-LANDS]** if the story is about **magical lands**.
- **[TOPIC-TIME-TRAVEL]**
  - A story is **[TOPIC-TIME-TRAVEL]** if the story is about **time travel**.

---

# 2) Composite Nodes (Built from Other Nodes)

## 2.1 Structural Profile
- **[STRUCTURAL-EPIC]**
  - A story is **[STRUCTURAL-EPIC]** if **both** of the following are true:
    - The story is **[LONG-STORY]**
    - The story is **[MANY-PARAGRAPHS]**

---

## 2.2 Voice / Opening Style
- **[EXPERIMENTAL-VOICE]**
  - A story is **[EXPERIMENTAL-VOICE]** if **either** of the following is true:
    - **Exactly one** of the following is true:
      - the story is **[STARTS-WITH-ADVERB]**
      - or the story is **[STARTS-WITH-ADJECTIVE]**
    - **Both** of the following are true:
      - The story is **[STARTS-WITH-NONNOUN]**
      - The story is **[USES-WH-QUESTIONS]**

---

## 2.3 Theme/Topic Fusion
- **[MYTHIC-ENGINEERING]**
  - A story is **[MYTHIC-ENGINEERING]** if **both** of the following are true:
    - **Either** of the following is true:
      - The story is **[THEME-MAGIC]**
      - The story is **[TOPIC-MAGICAL-LANDS]**
    - **Either** of the following is true:
      - The story is **[THEME-INNOVATION]**
      - The story is **[TOPIC-TIME-TRAVEL]**

---

## 2.4 Mood + Question/Complexity Interaction
- **[INTERROGATIVE-CONDITIONALITY]**
  - A story is **[INTERROGATIVE-CONDITIONALITY]** if **both** of the following are true:
    - The story is **[USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]**
    - **Either** of the following is true:
      - The story is **[USES-WH-QUESTIONS]**
      - The story is **[LOW-READABILITY]**

---

## 2.5 Embedded Plotting / Foreshadowing
- **[EMBEDDED-FORESHADOWING]**
  - A story is **[EMBEDDED-FORESHADOWING]** if **both** of the following are true:
    - The story is **[HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY]**
    - **Either** of the following is true:
      - The story is **[HAS-CHECKHOVS-GUN]**
      - **Both** of the following are true:
        - The story is **[USES-SUPERLATIVE-FORMS]**
        - It is **not [USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]**

---

## 2.6 Treasure + Character/Value Arc
- **[TREASURED-TRANSFORMATION]**
  - A story is **[TREASURED-TRANSFORMATION]** if **both** of the following are true:
    - The story is **[TOPIC-HIDDEN-TREASURES]**
    - **Either** of the following is true:
      - **Both** of the following are true:
        - The story is **[THEME-GROWTH]**
        - The story is **[HAS-A-MORAL-LESSON]**
      - **Both** of the following are true:
        - The story is **[THEME-HARDSHIP]**
        - It is **not [HAS-A-MORAL-LESSON]**

---

## 2.7 Seasons + Arts / Elevated Language (with readability constraint)
- **[SEASONAL-AESTHETIC]**
  - A story is **[SEASONAL-AESTHETIC]** if **both** of the following are true:
    - The story is **[TOPIC-SEASONAL-CHANGES]**
    - **Either** of the following is true:
      - The story is **[TOPIC-THE-ARTS]**
      - **Both** of the following are true:
        - The story is **[USES-SUPERLATIVE-FORMS]**
        - It is **not [LOW-READABILITY]**

---

# 3) Top-Level Synthesis Node

## 3.1 Final Classification
- **[MASTER-CLASS-PLOT]**
  - A story is **[MASTER-CLASS-PLOT]** if **both** of the following are true:
    - **Either** of the following is true:
      - The story is **[EMBEDDED-FORESHADOWING]**
      - **Both** of the following are true:
        - The story is **[STRUCTURAL-EPIC]**
        - The story is **[INTERROGATIVE-CONDITIONALITY]**
    - **Exactly one** of the following is true:
      - The story is **[TREASURED-TRANSFORMATION]**
      - The story is **[SEASONAL-AESTHETIC]**
