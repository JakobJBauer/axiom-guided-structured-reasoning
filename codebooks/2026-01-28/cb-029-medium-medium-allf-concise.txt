[STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL]
A story is [STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL] when it is philosophical.

[HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY]
A story is [HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY] when it contains a story within a story.

[USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]
A story is [USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD] when it uses the conditional mood.

[HAS-CIRCULAR-NARRATIVE-STRUCT]
A story is [HAS-CIRCULAR-NARRATIVE-STRUCT] when its narrative is circular.

[TOPIC-CULTURAL-TRADITIONS]
A story is [TOPIC-CULTURAL-TRADITIONS] when it concerns cultural traditions.

[SHORT-STORY]
A story is [SHORT-STORY] when it has fewer than 160 words.

[TOPIC-SEASONAL-CHANGES]
A story is [TOPIC-SEASONAL-CHANGES] when it concerns seasonal changes.

[LOW-READABILITY]
A story is [LOW-READABILITY] when its readability score is below 86.4.

[INNER-CONDITIONAL]
A story is [INNER-CONDITIONAL] if both are true:
- The story is [HAS-A-STORY-WITHIN-A-STORY]
- The story is [USES-CONDITIONAL-MOOD]

[PHILOS-CIRCULAR-XOR]
A story is [PHILOS-CIRCULAR-XOR] if both are true:
- Exactly one of the following is true:
  - The story is [STYLE-PHILOSOPHICAL]
  - The story is [HAS-CIRCULAR-NARRATIVE-STRUCT]
- The truth value of the following are equal:
  - [SHORT-STORY]
  - [LOW-READABILITY]

[THEMATIC-IN]
A story is [THEMATIC-IN] when its topic is one of:
- [TOPIC-CULTURAL-TRADITIONS]
- [TOPIC-SEASONAL-CHANGES]

[COMPLEX-NARRATIVE]
A story is [COMPLEX-NARRATIVE] if all of the following are true:
- Either of the following is true:
  - The story is [INNER-CONDITIONAL]
  - The story is [PHILOS-CIRCULAR-XOR]
- Both of the following are true:
  - The story is [THEMATIC-IN]
  - The story is not [SHORT-STORY]
