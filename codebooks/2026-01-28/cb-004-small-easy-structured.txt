## Codebook (Structured)

### 1) Topic Codes (What the story is about)

- **[TOPIC-SEASONAL-CHANGES]**
  - **Apply when:** The story is about **seasonal changes**.

- **[TOPIC-CULTURAL-TRADITIONS]**
  - **Apply when:** The story is about **cultural traditions**.

- **[TOPIC-TIME-TRAVEL]**
  - **Apply when:** The story is about **time travel**.


---

### 2) Form / Language Feature Codes (How the story is written)

- **[USES-WH-QUESTIONS]**
  - **Apply when:** The story uses **wh-questions** (e.g., who/what/when/where/why/how questions).

- **[STARTS-WITH-NOUN]**
  - **Apply when:** The story **starts with a noun**.


---

### 3) Composite (Logic-Based) Codes (Built from other codes)

- **[SEASONAL-QUESTION-STORY]**
  - **Apply when:** **Both** conditions below are true:
    - The story is **[TOPIC-SEASONAL-CHANGES]**
    - The story is **[USES-WH-QUESTIONS]**

- **[TRADITION-OR-TIME-STORY]**
  - **Apply when:** **Either** condition below is true:
    - The story is **[TOPIC-CULTURAL-TRADITIONS]**
    - The story is **[TOPIC-TIME-TRAVEL]**
