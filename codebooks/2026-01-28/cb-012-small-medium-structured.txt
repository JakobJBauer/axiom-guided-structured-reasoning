## Codebook (Structured)

### 1) Core Labels (Atomic Nodes)

#### [THEME-HELPING-OTHERS]
- **Use this label when:** the story has the theme **Helping Others**.

#### [TOPIC-MYSTICAL-CREATURES]
- **Use this label when:** the story is about **mystical creatures**.

#### [STARTS-WITH-PREPOSITION]
- **Use this label when:** the story **starts with a preposition**.

---

### 2) Composite Labels (Built from Other Nodes)

#### [MYTHIC-BUT-NOT-ALTRUISTIC]
- **Assign [MYTHIC-BUT-NOT-ALTRUISTIC] if and only if** *both* conditions below are true (**And**):
  - The story is **[TOPIC-MYSTICAL-CREATURES]**
  - The story is **not [THEME-HELPING-OTHERS]** (**Not**)

---

### 3) Higher-Level Composite Label

#### [INVITING-OPENING]
- **Assign [INVITING-OPENING] if and only if** *both* conditions below are true (**And**):
  - The story is **[STARTS-WITH-PREPOSITION]**
  - The story is **not [MYTHIC-BUT-NOT-ALTRUISTIC]** (**Not**)
