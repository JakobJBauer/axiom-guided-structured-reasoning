[STYLE-HUMOROUS]
Specification: A story is classified as [STYLE-HUMOROUS] if the story is humorous.

[STYLE-LIGHTHEARTED]
Specification: A story is classified as [STYLE-LIGHTHEARTED] if the story is lighthearted.

[THEME-KINDNESS]
Specification: A story is classified as [THEME-KINDNESS] if the story embodies the theme of kindness.

[STYLE-EXCLUSIVE]
Specification: A story is classified as [STYLE-EXCLUSIVE] if exactly one of the following conditions holds:
- The story is [STYLE-HUMOROUS]
- The story is [STYLE-LIGHTHEARTED]

Notes: This node implements an exclusive-or constraint between [STYLE-HUMOROUS] and [STYLE-LIGHTHEARTED]; membership in [STYLE-EXCLUSIVE] requires that precisely one of those style predicates evaluate to true.

[GENRE-GENTLE]
Specification: A story is classified as [GENRE-GENTLE] if both of the following conditions are satisfied:
- The story is [THEME-KINDNESS]
- Either of the following conditions hold:
  - The story is [STYLE-EXCLUSIVE]
  - Both of the following conditions hold:
    - The story is [STYLE-LIGHTHEARTED]
    - The story is not [STYLE-HUMOROUS]

Notes: [GENRE-GENTLE] requires thematic alignment with [THEME-KINDNESS] and a style constraint that is either the exclusive style option ([STYLE-EXCLUSIVE]) or a conjunctive requirement of being lighthearted without being humorous.
